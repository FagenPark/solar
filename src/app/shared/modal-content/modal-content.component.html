This estimate of your energy usage is based on the Australian Energy Regulator Electricity and gas bill benchmarks for residential customers 2017 and the Australian Energy Market Commission Residential Electricity Price Trends 2019. Our energy usage estimates assume no gas connection, no swimming pool and no underfloor heating. If your energy usage is more than our estimates you may consider installing a bigger sized solar system.

The kW size of a solar photovoltaic system is the maximum amount of electricity it can deliver. A system of this size should meet the typical energy needs of a household of your size. This estimate assumes you have enough roof space to install a system of this size.

This calculation estimates the period of time it will take you to completely pay off your solar system based on the energy savings you make in your home and the income you earn from feeding excess energy back to the grid.

Our calculation assumes that you have a north-facing rooftop with no tree shading and that you consume 25% of the solar energy you generate.
